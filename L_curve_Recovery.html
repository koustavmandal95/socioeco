L curve is More Real Why?

For V Curve Recovery there has to be a overwhelming pent up demand. Predominantly we are having a service based economy which means if someone is missing out on a dinner in a restaurant or a vacation trip these economic transaction loses are permanent but on other side if someone was planning to buy an electronics goods there can be pent up demand.But Would u fancy buying a luxurious car in middle of a pandemic.

Why L Shape Not V shape

There was huge gap in demand of goods and services due to slow economic growth. There is considerable amount of growth in GDP of countries around the world. Upon on this a Predominant fear of infection due to virus will definitely increase the umemployement .People are slowly moving into survial & saving mode rather than spending & expanding mode.

Why Helicopter-Money Bomb will fail.

In a crises situation any central bank around the world is under immense pressure by their respective government to increase the currency supply and reduce the repo rate to keep the value of paper assets up and let it not turn into junk bonds but printing money can't be the only solution of this problem because people are far from spending mode. Even though the value of paper currency is getting de-valued people are happy with their cash in their pockets in crises. So we are slowly moving towards a deflationary period where umemployement will be peeking and contraction of the economy. As a rule of economy someone's spending is someone's income. There is no point printing currency if the velocity of the currency is zero.
